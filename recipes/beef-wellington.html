Beef Wellington

Description

One of Gordon Ramsey's signature dishes, a beef wellington consists of a cut of beef that is coated in pate, wrapped in puff pastry, and baked until the pasty is a golden brown.

The dish is perfect for multiple occasions, from holidays to anniversaries. In total, this dish should take around an hour and 10 minutes to finish. Now let's get into the details!

Ingredients

  • 2.5 pounds of beef tenderloin
  • 4 tablespoons butter, softened, divided
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 onion
  • 0.5 cup sliced fresh mushrooms
  • 2 ounces liver pate
  • salt and pepper
  • 1 package frozen puff pastry
  • 1 large egg yolk
  • 1 can beef broth
  • 2 tablespoons of red wine

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C)
  2. Place beef tenderloin in baking dish and spread 2 tablespoons of softened butter over beef
  3. Bake 10-15 minutes until browned, removing beef and allowing it to cool
  4. Increase oven temperature to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C)
  5. Melt 2 tablespoons butter over medium heat, sauteing onions and mushrooms for 5 mins
  6. Mix pate and remaining butter in bowl, seasoned with salt and pepper. Spread mixture of beef and top with onions/mushrooms
  7. Roll puff pastry dough to 1/4 inch thickness and place beef in center
  8. Fold and seal, cut few slits, and brush with egg yolk
  9. Bake until the pastry is a rich, golden brown
  10. Meanwhile, place pan juices in a small saucepan over high heat to serve with beef
  11. Enjoy!
