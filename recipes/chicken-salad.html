Chicken Salad

Description

This is a healthy option that gets you good protein and fulfilling vegetables. This is also quick and basic, so it's great for leftovers.

This takes very few ingredients and 10 minutes of your time. Everyone should have this in their locker, and here's how to make it.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of chopped cooked chicken
  • 0.5 cups mayo
  • 1 chopped stalk of celery
  • 0.5 cup almonds
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 0.25 teaspoon ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Toast almonds
  2. Mix mayos, lemon juice, and pepper
  3. Toss chicken with mixture, almonds, and celery
  4. Enjoy!
