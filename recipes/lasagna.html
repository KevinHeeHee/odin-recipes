Lasagna

Description

This recipe has been one of the most popular recipes on the site "allrecipes" for two decades. It also racks up more than 7 million views per year.

The time to make lasagna can be quite long, but it will be worth it! Let's see what all the hype is about.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato sauce
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasoning
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheese
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let lasagna rest before serving
